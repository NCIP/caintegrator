Selecting Samples of Interest in a Plot
To select the samples of interest in an I-SPY plot, follow these steps:
1.
Click and drag a rectangle around the samples.
A red rectangle appears around the samples, and the list of the samples appears on the right-hand side.
2.
To help lasso the points on the plot and identify the location of these points, mouse over a sample name in the list.
A yellow circle appears on the plot where the sample is located.
3.
To generate clinical data for the selected samples and save the samples, click the view clinical data link. See Clinical Reports.
To select another group of samples, click the clear samples link and start again.
