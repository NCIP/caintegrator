caIntegrator2 Functions
When you log into caIntegrator2, the workspace opens to one study, whose name is listed at the top of the left sidebar.
You can access all caIntegrator2 functions from the sidebar on the left side of the page.
The following table describes each caIntegrator2 option in the workspace .

[Study Name]
When you log in, this study displays in the left sidebar by default. Any study that you select in the My Studies drop-down list in the upper right of the page replaces this default selection.
