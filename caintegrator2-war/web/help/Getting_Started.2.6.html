caIntegrator2 Functions
When you log into caIntegrator2, the workspace opens to the Rembrandt/Vasari study, which displays as the default.
You can access all caIntegrator2 functions from the sidebar on the left side of the page.The following table describes each caIntegrator2 option on the workspace .

[Rembrandt/Vasari]
When you log in, this study displays in the left sidebar by default. Any study that you select in the My Studies drop-down list in the upper right of the page replaces this default selection.

In the My Studies drop-down list in the upper right of the page, select the study you want to use for your current session. (The list includes all studies to which you are subscribed.) As you do so, the left sidebar contents change to reflect options relevant to your study selection.
- the list of saved queries for that study
- the logo for the selected study (if it exists)
- the name for the selected study
