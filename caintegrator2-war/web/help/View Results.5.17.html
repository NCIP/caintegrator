Accessing an NCIA Image from a Clinical Report
To access an NCIA image from a clinical report, follow these steps:
Click the NCIA icon in the NCIA Image column.The NCIA web site appears. As a first time user, register with NCIA to obtain a username and password. Then you can log in and have access to these images.
