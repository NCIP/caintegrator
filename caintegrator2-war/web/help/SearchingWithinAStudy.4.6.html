Searching a caIntegrator2 Study > Manage Queries
Manage Queries
Saving a Query
1.
Click the Save As tab and enter a Search Name and Search Description, unique to the search. Example: Batch ID 6 and female
2.
Check the appropriate options to save the column and sort selections you have defined for the search.
3.
Click Save.
Once the query is saved, it is listed by its name under the Study Data > Queries > My Queries in the left sidebar, whenever the study to which the query applies is selected in caIntegrator2.
