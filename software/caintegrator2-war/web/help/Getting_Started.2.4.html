Logging In
To log into caIntegrator2, follow these steps:
1.
On the login page, enter your username and password.
2.
Click the Login button. If your login is successful, the Welcome to Browse/Study page appears (1.1).
See also Registering as a New caIntegrator2 User on page 6 and Using the caIntegrator2 Workspace.
