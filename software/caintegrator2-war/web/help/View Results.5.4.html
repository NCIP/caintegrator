Browse Clinical Data Results
Initially, the entire list of study images will be displayed. The research investigator may view complete details for a single subject. Details shown for the subject include:
•
All clinical fields that the Research Investigator is permitted to see.
•
All samples associated to the subject that the Research Investigator is permitted to see. Selecting a sample will display the detail information described below in Browse Genomic Data.
•
All images associated to the subject organized by series. Selecting an image will display the detail information described below in Browse Imaging Data.
•
Click Export Options CSV link to download the CSV file whose data displays on the Search Results tab. When you do so, the CSV file opens automatically in MS Excel (or???), showing the columns and sorting as you defined them in caIntegrator on the appropriate tabs.
